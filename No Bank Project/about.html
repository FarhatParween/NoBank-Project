About Us

Company Overview


NoBank is a UK-based Online Bank, Providing all money management solutions in one Application, Making transactions easier for everyone across the world at their fingertips. The company's products and features are based on Algorithms and Artificial intelligence, making users adapt to the New and Improved way of Banking. Nobank is made for everyone, bringing home users to international users altogether. University admissions and students relocating to other countries will become hassle-free.

Our Vision


We will be a technology and app-based solution provider for all age groups, millennials to elders it's built for everyone to save, invest, and trade money. Our vision is to become one single enterprise for banking products and services by filling the gaps left by the current industry. In the current market, most of the banks and tech companies tend to collect information from the end-users which is not relevant to their offered product or services. However, Nobank will be addressing all the customer's issue on one single platform by protecting the end user’s data. We are going to be Uber-like on-demand solution for Banking.
